Just found this little nugget crawling through garybernhardt's .vimrc. This baby makes tab autocomplete intelligently, i.e. if you aren't at the beginning of a line or a space. {% highlight text %} " Indent if we're at the beginning of a line. Else, do completion. function! InsertTabWrapper() let col = col('.') - 1 if !col || getline('.')[col - 1] !~ '\k' return "\" else return "\" endif endfunction inoremap =InsertTabWrapper() inoremap {% endhighlight %}
